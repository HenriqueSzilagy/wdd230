Contrast

apple.com/

Alignment

Microsoft

microsoft.com/pt-br

We can also notice on the microsoft website that there is a good alignment between the images with the text, and the lines of texts with each other, this makes it pleasant to read and gives order to everything

Rule of Thirds

Netflix

netflix.com/

already on Netflix we can notice that the first thing our eyes are directed to is "Unlimited Movies, TV Shows and more." then the email address box etc, this is a very good example of a visual hierarchy, they guide us to what they want us to see
